Energy Source Breakdown by Continent
Overall, Asia produces the highest amount of energy across all continents.

Energy consumption is linked to the population and enconomy.

So higher the population of a continent or larger the enconomy, energy consumption tends to increase.
GDP Per Capita Vs. Energy Consumption
There is a positive correlation between the GDP and total energy Produced .

Countries like India and China produce the highest amount of energy despite having a small GDP per capita.

This is due to the fact that they have very large populations requiring more energy
Energy Consumption Growth by Source
Overall, There is a constant increase in energy consumed over the years.

There is a sudden dip in energy consumed duing the years 2008 and 2019

This was caused by the financial crisis of 2008 and then the pandemic during 2019 reducing global demand for energy.
